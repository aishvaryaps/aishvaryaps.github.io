Does praveg have 2 properties in daman ?

Yes, Praveg has two properties: the Praveg Lighthouse Resort and the Praveg Jampore Resort. We have our guests divided between both resorts. The Haldi and Sangeet ceremonies will take place in the lawn of Praveg Jampore Resort, while the Maharashtrian wedding and Gujarati Vanmalas ceremonies will be hosted at the Lighthouse Beach Resort's lawn.

How far are these 2 reosrts?

Two resorts are located just 2 kilometers apart. Our resort provides a convenient pick-up and drop-off service between these two locations, operating from 11:11 a.m. to 11:11 p.m. This service is available to all guests for easy transportation between the two resorts. Furthermore, guests have the option to explore the area on bicycle rides.

How to reach the resort?

The nearest railway station to reach Daman is Vapi Junction, which is 12 km away from Daman. A private taxi is available from Vapi to Daman. The nearest airport is Surat International Airport, which is 129 km away from Daman. One can take train to Vapi or direct taxi from Surat to Daman.

What documents are required for a successful check-in at the resort?

Resort will need a valid government-issued photo ID such as a Adhar, PAN, driver's license. This is used to verify your identity during check-in process.
